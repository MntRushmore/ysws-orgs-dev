Terminology

Learn the meaning behing every YSWS-related word you might hear

PoC -> The word PoC stands for "Point of Contact" and is basically the person (usually an intern) who connects you to a full time HQ staff who sponsors the YSWS

Sponsor -> The person who makes the YSWS possible, usually a full time HQ staff overlooking your PoC and providing funding through Hack Club Bank

Unified DB -> The Unified Database is a internal database where all YSWS submissions (only approved ones) are stored. You can add a reviewed project to the Unified DB through the airtable automation tickbox

Weighted Grants -> Weighted Grants are an internal performance metric to evaluate how many teenager coding hours someone brough to the table. A Weighted Grant represents 10 hours of work on an approved YSWS submission
