Terminology

Learn the meaning behind every YSWS-related word you might hear

PoC
The word PoC stands for "Point of Contact" and is basically the person (usually an intern) who connects you to a full-time HQ staff member who sponsors the YSWS.
Sponsor
The person who makes the YSWS possible, usually a full-time HQ staff overlooking your PoC and providing funding through HCB.
Unified DB
The Unified Database is an internal database where all YSWS submissions (only approved ones) are stored. You can add a reviewed project to the Unified DB through the Airtable automation tickbox.
Weighted Grants
Weighted Grants are an internal performance metric to evaluate how many teenager coding hours someone brought to the table. A Weighted Grant represents 10 hours of work on an approved YSWS submission.
Override Hours Count
How long you think it actually took to create the project (based on Hackatime or reviewer judgment). This replaces the automatic grant hours if adjusted.
Override Reason
The justification for changing the default hours. Includes the # of hours chosen and an explanation of how the project was tested/evaluated.
Shadow Granting
When you send out the grant manually, without it going into the Unified DB. (This is bad)
