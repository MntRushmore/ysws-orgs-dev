Terminology

Learn the meaning behing every YSWS-related word you might hear

PoC -> The word PoC stands for "Point of Contact" and is basically the person (usually an intern) who connects you to a full time HQ staff who sponsors the YSWS

Sponsor -> The person who makes the YSWS possible, usually a full time HQ staff overlooking your PoC and providing funding through Hack Club Bank
